Parading, sculpture, and spectacle are among the oldest of community-driven collaborations and often involve impressive feats of design, engineering, and logistics. Kinetic Arts & Crafts is a program run in partnership with the Eliot School hoping to capture a bit of that history and bring it to Boston area youth. In it, participants combine kinetic sculpture & float design over eight weeks, culminating in a sidewalk spectacle and parade through Jamaica Plain's downtown.
