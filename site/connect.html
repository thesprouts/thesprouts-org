Our Studios

Our studios are a place for us to prototype our projects and programs. They have a mix of light manufacturing, arts & crafts, wet lab, and electrical engineering equipment.

This past year we welcomed Parts & Crafts to share our space as they experiment with their Center for Semi-Conducted Learning. Between that and pending renovations, we've temporarily closed our studios to drop-in use 'til next spring. But, if you think they'd be useful to you—especially if you've got a meetup or event or community group looking for a venue—let us know!

Support sprout

If you like what sprout does and would like to get involved, drop us a line. We always have a few projects in the hopper that could use a hand. We're especially keen to hear from people interested in coordinating or designing programs & workshops in the spirit of our past work.

If you like what sprout does and would like to make a [tax-deductible] donation, great! We are a registered, 501(c)3 nonprofit and would love to hear from you. Or if you're ready to donate, just enter your information below and we'll email you a receipt and be in touch. If you have any questions or if you've some other type of donation (e.g. equipment) to make, drop us a line.
