More than four dozen volunteers came together over a couple months to design & construct the streetbeest, a twelve-foot tall, twenty-foot wide walking sculpture inspired by the work of Dutch artist and engineer Theo Jansen. This project was an experiment in collaboration, community-driven design, and an exploration of two, storied art forms: mechanical linkages & parades. Months of work culminated in a mile long march from Davis to Harvard Square as part of the 2010 HONK! Parade.
