Our Studios

Our studios are a place for us to prototype our projects and programs. They have a mix of light manufacturing, basic wet lab, electrical engineering, and arts & crafts supplies and equipment.

In the past, we’ve opened them up to the community as much is convenient. This past year we welcomed Parts & Crafts to share our space for a few months as they experiment with their Semiconducted Learning Center. Between that and pending renovations, we’ve temporarily closed our studios to drop-in, public use. We plan to re-open them next spring; if you’re interested in staying abreast of the schedule, sign up for our newsletter or follow @sprouts.

That said, if you think they’d be useful to you—especially if you’ve got a meetup or event or community group looking for a venue—let us know! We’d love to help out.

Support sprout

If you like what sprout does and would like to lend a hand, drop us a line. We always have a few projects in the hopper that could use a hand. We’re especially keen to hear from people interested in co-teaching or co-designing programs & workshops in the spirit of our past work.

If you like what sprout does and would like to make a [tax-deductible] donation, great! We’re a registered, 501(c)3 nonprofit. Just enter your information below and we’ll email you a receipt and be in touch. If you’ve some other type of donation to make drop us a line.
